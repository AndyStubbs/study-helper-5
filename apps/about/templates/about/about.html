About Study Helper

Study Helper is an AI-powered tool designed to assist you in your learning journey. It offers various features to enhance your study experience:

  • Topic Generator: Get AI-generated descriptions and related topics for any subject you're studying.
  • Quiz: Test your knowledge with AI-generated multiple-choice questions based on your chosen topic.
  • Analytics: Track your progress and see your proficiency in various topics and concepts.

To get started, simply navigate to the Topic Generator tab, enter a topic you'd like to study, and let our AI assist you in exploring and understanding the subject matter.
